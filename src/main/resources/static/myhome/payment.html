About Us

My HOME has started it's roots in 2021 when company was founded in Rochester and relocated to New York in 2021. It's ran by enthusiasts and professionals in urban design field and has grown from a small retail store to distributor and dealer for well known brands that are aiming for quality and trust. Our company is offering wide and special range of products. We have been exploring the world and traveled a lot to see what home design is about and where we can meet our demand - and here we are!.
