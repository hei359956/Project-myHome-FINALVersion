About Us

My HOME has its beginnings in 2000, when it was founded in New York and moved to Bern (Switzerland) in 2021. It is run by enthusiasts and professionals in the field of urban design and has grown from a small retail store to a distributor and retailer of well-known brands that focus on quality and trust. Our company offers a wide and specialized range of products. We have travelled the world to see what home design is all about and where we can satisfy our needs - and here we are!.
